# Privacy Policy for Spidey LM

**Last updated:** March 30, 2026

## Overview

Spidey LM is a Chrome extension that runs entirely on your local device. We are committed to protecting your privacy by design.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Spidey LM:

- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not require user accounts
- Does not store data in the cloud

## Data Processing

All data processing happens locally on your device:

- **Page content** is processed by your local Ollama instance and never leaves your machine
- **Chat history** is stored only in your browser's local session and cleared when you close the popup
- **Settings** (theme, model selection) are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device

## Third-Party Services

Spidey LM connects only to:

- **localhost (127.0.0.1)** - Your local Ollama instance for AI processing

No external APIs, cloud services, or third-party servers are contacted.

## Permissions

The extension requests these Chrome permissions:

- **activeTab** - To read the content of the current tab when you click a quick action
- **storage** - To save your preferences (theme, model) locally

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is processed by your local Ollama installation, your data security is determined by your own device security.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at: https://github.com/bhushan23/spidey-lm/issues

## Summary

**Your data stays on your device. Period.**
